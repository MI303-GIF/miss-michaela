IMMUNE SYSTEM DISODERS

More than 15 primary defects and over 30 other conditions with secondary depression of neutrophil function. E.g. Neutrophil hypomotility –actin in neutrophils does not polymerize normally. Chronic granulomatous disease – there is failure to generate O-2 in both neutrophils and Monocytes and consequent inability to kill phagocytosed bacteria.

MECHANISMS OF ACTION

1.Binding of IgE antibodies to mast cells and circulating basophils.

2.Activation and degranulation of mast cells and basophils.

3.Activation of phospholipase A2 and C.

4.Degranulation releases mediators histamine, heparin, neutrophil chemotactic factor, and proteases.
